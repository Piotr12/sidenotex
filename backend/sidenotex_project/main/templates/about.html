What is sidenotex?

sidenotex (side notes extension) is a collaborative web annotation tool that lets teams share notes and insights about web pages within their organization. Think of it as shared margins for the web, where colleagues can leave thoughts and comments that are visible to everyone in their company domain.

How it works

The main interface to sidenotex is our Chrome Extension, which lets you:

  • (1) Add annotations to any webpage in a click of a button
  • (2) See your colleagues' annotations as you browse
  • (1+2=3) Share insights and knowledge within your organization

Privacy & Sharing

Your annotations are only presented to others in your organization (those with the same email domain). This creates a safe space for internal knowledge sharing while maintaining privacy between different organizations.
